Pastel de nata

Description

Pastel de nata is a Portuguese egg tart pastry that has a creamy custard filling and a flaky crust, often enjoyed with a sprinkle of cinnamon or powdered sugar.

It is a beloved treat in Portugal and has gained popularity worldwide, known for its rich flavor and delightful texture.

    Ingredients
  • 1sheet of puff pastry (store-bought or homemade)
  • 250 ml (1 cup) whole milk
  • g (2 tbsp) all-purpose flour
  • 50 g (1¼ cups) granulated sugar
  • 125 ml (½ cup) water
  • 6 large egg yolks
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 1 strip of lemon peel (just the yellow part)

    Instructions

  1. Make the sugar syrup
  2. Make the milk-flour base
  3. Combine syrup and milk base
  4. Add egg yolks
  5. Prepare the pastry
  6. Fill and bake
  7. Cool and serve
