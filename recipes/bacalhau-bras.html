Bacalhau à Brás

Description

Bacalhau à Brás is a classic Portuguese dish made with shredded salted cod, onions, and thinly cut fried potatoes, all bound together with scrambled eggs and garnished with parsley and black olives.

This comforting and flavorful recipe is popular throughout Portugal and is known for its creamy texture and savory taste.

Ingredients

  • 400g salted cod (bacalhau), soaked and shredded
  • 500g potatoes, cut into matchsticks
  • 1 large onion, thinly sliced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 6 eggs
  • Olive oil, for frying
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Chopped fresh parsley, for garnish
  • Black olives, for garnish

Instructions

  1. Soak the salted cod in water for 24-48 hours, changing the water several times. Drain and shred the cod.
  2. Fry the potato matchsticks in olive oil until golden and crispy. Drain on paper towels.
  3. In a large pan, sauté the onion and garlic in olive oil until soft.
  4. Add the shredded cod and cook for a few minutes, stirring well.
  5. Add the fried potatoes and mix gently.
  6. Beat the eggs and pour them into the pan, stirring constantly until just set but still creamy.
  7. Season with salt and pepper. Garnish with chopped parsley and black olives. Serve immediately.
